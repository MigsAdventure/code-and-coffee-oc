Who We Are

Whether you are a total beginner or well experienced, we welcome you to join us if you are interested in code pairing,enhancing your technical skills in JavaScript, JQuery, HTML, CSS, Angular.js, Node.js. and much more.

We started this for anyone currently taking the Free Code Camp courses online at Freecodecamp hoping to connect with a local campers in Orange County, CA, but have expanded to those interested in learning other programming languages and related technical skills.

If you would like to host an event or have a venue in mind, feel free to reach out!

Find us on Meetup or join us on Slack.
